When a patient needs a person

Your receptionist can’t transfer a call or a chat. What it can do is stop trying and promise that someone from your clinic will get back to them.

When on, your receptionist is told: “If the customer asks for a human, is upset, or you cannot help, offer a callback from clinic staff.” When off, it keeps trying to help on its own.

The people who return these calls. Include the country code. Your receptionist can’t go live without at least one.

Nothing rings these numbers automatically. They’re your record of who handles callbacks. To answer a patient yourself, message TAKEOVER <their number> to your clinic’s WhatsApp — your receptionist steps aside until you send DONE.

If someone describes an emergency
Your receptionist always tells them to call emergency services immediately.

That instruction is built in and can’t be edited or switched off. Anything you add below is given as well — never instead of it.

One sentence, in your words — where to go, or who to ask for. Leave it blank and your receptionist simply gives the emergency-services instruction on its own.
